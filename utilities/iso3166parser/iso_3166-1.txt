
In a few cases the present entries are left unchanged by the patch
despite the fact that the official 'Country names (short names)' 
from ISO 3166-1 are different, because the official short names 
are unnecessarily long:
FM      Micronesia (official short name: Micronesia, Federated States of)
IR      Iran (official short name: Iran, Islamic Republic of)
LA      Laos (official short name: Lao People's Democratic Republic)
LY      Libya (official short name: Libyan Arab Jamahiriya)
MK      Macedonia (official short name: Macedonia, The Former Yugoslav Republic of)
PS      Palestine (official short name: Palestinian Territory, Occupied)
SY      Syria (official short name: Syrian Arab Republic)
TW      Taiwan (official short name: Taiwan, Province of China)
TZ      Tanzania (official short name: Tanzania, United Republic of)

The /usr/share/zoneinfo/iso3166.tab file says that names differing 
from the official ISO 3166-1 names were used in some cases to get a better 
alphabetic sorting. However, the changes below will not lead to
any problems with respect to a meaningful alphabetic sorting by 
country name (as far as I can see).
